Projects

Some of my projects

  • Bevy Snake (code) - A snake implementation written in Rust using the Bevy game engine.
  • Climate Dash (code) - My attempt to create a dashboard with visualizations of the weather and climate change at places near to me.
  • Crop Notes - My attempt to document information on different crops that I have grown.
  • Sunson Farms - Data from running my natural, minimal fossil fuel, farming practice
  • Sophia Publishing - My attempt to typeset and publish books using LaTeX.
  • Precalculus - LaTeX source of the book by Stitz Zeager, formatted for a6 paper that can be read on small screen ereaders.
  • Markets Dash - Dashboard to track markets or what we incorrectly call the economy.
  • The Unofficial Directory - Government of India - My attempt at collating the contact information and power structure of the people who serve and govern India.
